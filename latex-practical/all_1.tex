\section{Mathematical Equation}
The GDP growth rate ($g$) can be calculated as:
\[
g = \frac{GDP_t - GDP_{t-1}}{GDP_{t-1}} \times 100
\]
For example, if GDP in 2021 was 210 and in 2020 was 200:
\[
g = \frac{210 - 200}{200} \times 100 = 5\%
\]
